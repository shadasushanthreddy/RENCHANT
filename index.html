Who We Are

Renchant Accommodations is a data driven housing rental service platform. It's vision is to solve the long-term and short-term rental apartment problem in India. It is committed to transforming the traditional housing rental industry with the use of ML, AI and data driven models. Founded in early 2019, the company is a data-driven, high-quality rental, asset management and residential service platform. The product form covers shared apartments and rented apartments to meet the diverse residential needs of techies in the city.
